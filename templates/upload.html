CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file should contain these columns:

  • bedrooms
  • bathrooms
  • sqft_living
  • sqft_lot
  • floors
  • waterfront
  • view
  • condition
  • grade
  • sqft_above
  • sqft_basement
  • yr_built
  • yr_renovated
  • lat
  • long
  • sqft_living15
  • sqft_lot15
Note: The 'price' column is not needed as we'll predict it for you.
